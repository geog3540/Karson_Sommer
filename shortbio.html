Karson Sommer Graduate Teaching Assistant

I am a graduate student in the business analytics program. My undergraduate degree was in electrical engineering. My focus area in my coursework has been digital signal processing (DSP) and digital image processing (DIP). Subject matters that I am interested in include medical image analysis, GNSS and biometric data associated with running/cycling, and feature extraction from satellite imagery. A summary of some of my previous work can be found at https://karsonkevin2.github.io/Karson-Sommer/ I plan on pursuing a PhD related to one of my subject interests.

In my free time I enjoy running, contributing information to OpenStreetMap, and playing videogames.
